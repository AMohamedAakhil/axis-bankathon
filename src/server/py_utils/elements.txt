1) Job Purpose:A concise overview of the role's purpose and 
                primary responsibilities. 

2)Key Responsibilities: A detailed list of tasks, duties, 
                        and responsibilities that the employee will be expected to perform. 
                        This provides clarity on what the role entails.

3)Qualifications:This section lists the required level of job knowledge 
              (such as education, experience, knowledge, skills, and abilities)
               required to do the job. This section focuses on the “minimum” level 
               of qualifications for an individual to be productive and successful 
               in this role.

4)Location:This field indicates where the job is based.

5)Salary and Benefits:Compensation details and any offered benefit

6)Reporting Structure: All the reporting relationships has to clarfied, 
                      including who the employee will report to and who will report to them, 
                      if applicable.
